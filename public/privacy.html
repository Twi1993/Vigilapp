Privacy Policy

Vigil+ — Vigil Studios
Last updated: April 24, 2026

Overview

Vigil+ is a time-aware daily companion app based on the Liturgy of the Hours. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

Vigil+ does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app does not require you to create an account.

Push Notifications

Vigil+ uses Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver push notifications. A device token is generated to enable notifications. This token is not linked to any personal information and is not stored or shared by Vigil Studios.

Local Storage

The app stores minimal data locally on your device (such as whether you have completed onboarding) using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Vigil+ uses Google Firebase for push notification delivery. Firebase's privacy policy can be found at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

Children's Privacy

Vigil+ is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
